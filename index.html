    gScholarAnalysis

    I saw this post a few weeks ago on R-bloggers. It was an interesting idea of pulling down your Google Scholar data, processing it some and extracting interesting bits about it. The post that inspired this person is here, wherein he pulled down his data and made a simple plot showing how many publications he had per year since the first one. It's an interesting proof-of-concept, but this plot already prominently sites on your Google Scholar profile page. What interested me was the co-author index and the word cloud from your publications' titles.
